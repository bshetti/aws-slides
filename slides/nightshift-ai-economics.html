AI Economics
Token efficiency is a core differentiator.
You can't pass petabytes of raw logs to an LLM. The platform that compresses signal without losing meaning wins: on quality and cost.
Raw Telemetry
Logs, metrics, traces — logs are the primary GTM signal
Petabytes Never passed to LLM directly. Stored at low cost, queried on demand via ES|QL.
↓ KI Extractor distills signal
Knowledge Indicators
Entities, dependencies, state, context — structured system understanding
Megabytes The right context, structured. Agents reason over this layer for investigation.
↓ Investigation correlates
Significant Events
Correlated situations with RCA, blast radius, severity — what actually matters
Kilobytes Precise context for action. This is what gets passed to the LLM and the human.
The hierarchy IS the token strategy. Each layer compresses meaning while discarding noise. Agents reason over Significant Events and KIs — not raw logs. This is what makes Elastic's AI economically viable at enterprise scale.
